Hello, World!

Hi there! This is my first post on this blog. In the absence of any classes, exams, clubs, and other normal scholarly obligations, I've come to an overdue realization this winter break: I like to write. It's one of my favorite activities, in fact. There's something uniquely satisfying about constructing coherent, concise, and pretty sentences. Without any upcoming essays looming over my head, I find myself writing longer, more reflective texts to my friends, redirecting all my creative juices to just about any trivial conversation.

Realizing this curious tendency begged the question: why don't I journal? Turns out, there's an unfortunate answer: if I'm not doing something CS-y or career-y or school-y, I don't feel productive. So I made it productive! By turning this hobby into a resume item, my hope is that I'll be more motivated to write out my thoughts.

So, was this blog born out of an unhealthy relationship with productivity? Perhaps. And did I spend time on this site just so I could feel good about writing? Partially. But while we're here, let me introduce myself. I'm Aagney, I'm currently 18 years old, and I love soccer. I was born in India, grew up on the Eastside, went to Juanita High School in Kirkland, WA, and now attend the Unviersity of Washington. I'm a computer science major, and I'm learning new things about the field every day. Welcome to my website!

tl;dr: This blog was made so that I could turn a hobby of mine - writing - into a productive frontend web design project. Thanks for visiting!
